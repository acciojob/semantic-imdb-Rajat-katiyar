Summary

Inception is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan that explores the concept of shared dreams and the ability to plant ideas in someone’s subconscious. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. Given a chance at redemption, he must perform the seemingly impossible "Inception"—planting an idea into a target's mind.
